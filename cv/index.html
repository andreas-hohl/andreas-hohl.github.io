Curriculum Vitæ

Academic Positions

Since October 2023, I have been a postdoc at the Department of Mathematics of Katholieke Universiteit Leuven in the group of Nero Budur.

Between 2021 and 2023, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Institut de Mathématiques de Jussieu-Paris Rive Gauche (IMJ-PRG, France), hosted by Philip Boalch and funded by a Walter Benjamin scholarship of the German Research Foundation (DFG).

In the academic year 2020/21, I was a research associate at Chemnitz University of Technology in the group of Christian Sevenheck.

Before, I was a PhD student at the University of Augsburg at the Chair of Algebra and Number Theory, advised by Marco Hien.

In 2013, I spent an ERASMUS semester at the University of Glasgow (Scotland, UK).

In 2018, I was a guest of Andrea D’Agnolo for a research stay at the University of Padova (Italy).

During my PhD, I was funded by a doctoral scholarship of the Studienstiftung des deutschen Volkes (German Academic Scholarship Foundation). In Augsburg and Munich, I was part of the TopMath elite study programme.
