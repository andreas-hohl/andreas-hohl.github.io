Teaching

I am currently teaching exercise classes for the following courses at Chemnitz University of Technology:

  • Funktionentheorie (Complex Analysis)
  • Höhere Mathematik I (Further Mathematics)

During my time in Augsburg, I was responsible for the following courses (mostly exercise classes):

  • Winter 2019/20: Einführung in die Algebra (Introductory course in Algebra/Galois theory)
  • Summer 2019: Lineare Algebra 1
  • Winter 2018/19: Kommutative Algebra
  • Summer 2018: Komplexe Geometrie (Complex Geometry)
  • Winter 2017/18: Kommutative Algebra
  • Winter 2016/17 and Summer 2017: Algebraische Topologie (Algebraic Topology, two-semester course)
  • Summer 2016: Seminar zu De-Rham-Kohomologie (Seminar on De Rham cohomology)
  • Winter 2015/16: Analysis 3 (Measure and Integration Theory)

I also completed the Certificate for Teaching in Higher Education of the Bavarian Universities.

Moreover, I was involved in the Matheschülerzirkel project at the University of Augsburg, giving particularly interested pupils the opportunity to dive deeper into mathematics.
